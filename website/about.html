About us

Built to keep your presence alive, without the busywork.

Who we are

AutoPilot is developed and operated by Kushal Budhathoki, an independent developer building tools for creators and engineering teams who want to stay visible online without spending hours every week on manual posting, scheduling, and replying.

What we do

AutoPilot connects to your GitHub activity, social accounts, and content sources to generate AI-written captions, schedule posts at the right time, and draft replies to Instagram DMs and comments for human review. It's built as a single workspace so automation stays predictable and every action is easy to audit or undo.

Our approach to privacy

We only request the account permissions each feature actually needs, we don't sell user data, and we give users clear controls to disconnect accounts or delete stored data at any time. Full details are in our Privacy Policy.
